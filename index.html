FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the task, what sort of technicality the project will have, your design needs, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to deliver?

The time limit of any project is normally dictated by the client and complexity. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the making of a project is waiting for the materials and products to be sent to us by our supplier.

+

How do I associate with BONWAKS Company?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of development.

+

Can you help my current place look more professional?

Yes. Give us your requirements and we have experienced expertise to help you give a new professional look that really wonders!

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept cheques, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
